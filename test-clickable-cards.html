✅ Implementation Status

All product cards have been updated to be clickable! Users can now click anywhere on a product card to go to the product detail page.

📋 What Was Changed

  • category.js - Product cards now have onclick handlers and removed "View Details" button
  • main.js - Featured product cards are now clickable with event listeners
  • subcategory.js - Subcategory product cards are now clickable
  • style.css - Added hover effects and cursor styles for clickable cards

🧪 Test the Implementation

Click on any of the links below to test the clickable product cards on different pages:

🏠 Home Page (Featured Products) 📚 Collections Page 👕 Category Page (Sneakers) 👟 Subcategory Page

🎯 How to Test

  1. Go to any of the pages above
  2. Look for product cards (they should show product image, name, and price)
  3. Click anywhere on the product card (not on buttons)
  4. You should be redirected to product-detail.html?id=[product_id]
  5. Buttons (Add to Cart, Quick View, etc.) should still work independently

🔧 Technical Details

  • Event Handling: Cards use onclick or addEventListener for click events
  • Button Protection: event.stopPropagation() prevents card clicks when clicking buttons
  • Visual Feedback: Hover effects with transform and box-shadow
  • Cursor: Cards show pointer cursor, buttons maintain their own cursor

📱 Responsive Design

The clickable cards work on all device sizes and maintain the existing responsive grid layout.
